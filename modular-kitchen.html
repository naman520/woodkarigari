FAQ

Modular Kitchen Questions Answered

What is the best wood for a modular kitchen in Delhi? +

BWR-grade plywood is the best choice for kitchen carcasses because it handles humidity, termites and daily kitchen usage better than basic MR-grade plywood.

How long does modular kitchen installation take? +

A standard modular kitchen usually takes 15–25 working days from design approval to installation. Island kitchens or complex layouts can take around 30 days.

Do you provide a 3D design before ordering? +

Yes. A free 3D kitchen design visualization is included so you can see the layout, finishes and storage before production starts.

Is Hettich or Blum hardware better? +

Both are good German hardware brands. Hettich is usually more budget-friendly, while Blum is known for very smooth operation and premium feel.
